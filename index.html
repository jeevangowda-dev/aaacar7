BASIC TAXI

Our Basic Taxi service is ideal for everyday travel and short-distance journeys. It offers a reliable, safe, and affordable way to get around the city. All vehicles are clean and well-maintained, driven by professional drivers who ensure timely pickups and smooth rides. This service is perfect for daily commuting, quick errands, and budget-friendly travel needs.

STANDARD TAXI

Our Standard Taxi service provides extra comfort and convenience for passengers who want a better travel experience. It is well-suited for airport transfers, business trips, and longer journeys. With spacious vehicles, professional drivers, and a smooth ride, this service offers the perfect balance between comfort, quality, and value.

7 SEATER

Our 7-seater taxi is perfect for families, small groups, or travelers carrying extra luggage. It offers a smooth and comfortable ride, making it ideal for airport transfers, group trips, and long-distance travel. With spacious seating, ample luggage space, and professional drivers, every journey is safe, relaxed, and on time.

LUXURIOUS TAXI

Our Luxurious Taxi service delivers a premium travel experience with high-end vehicles and professional chauffeurs. Designed for corporate travel, special occasions, and VIP transfers, this service offers superior comfort, style, and attention to detail. It is ideal for those who want elegance, privacy, and first-class service.
